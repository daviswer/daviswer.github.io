I am a Ph.D. student at Cornell University, Ithaca campus. My research is in Machine Learning and Computer Vision, with a focus on few-shot visual understanding. Since high-quality training data for a given task may not always be readily available in the wild, I believe it is important to understand how neural models generalize and adapt to new domains from partially relevant data or indirect supervision.

Bharath Hariharan is my advisor.
